        <div class="tip-icon">🚄</div>
        <div class="tip-title">Train Tickets: Don't Do What I Did</div>
        <p class="tip-text">I often booked high-speed rail tickets the night before departure. As a solo traveller, single seats are sometimes easier to secure — but this is genuinely risky during peak season. Plan ahead if you can.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="tip-card">
        <div class="tip-icon">📶</div>
        <div class="tip-title">Get Both SIM + eSIM</div>
        <p class="tip-text">I bought both. A physical Chinese SIM makes it easier to communicate with delivery riders and Didi drivers. An eSIM gives you easy access to international websites. Buy your SIM in the city centre — airport SIMs are more expensive.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="tip-card">
        <div class="tip-icon">💳</div>
        <div class="tip-title">Alipay Foreign Card Fees</div>
        <p class="tip-text">Foreign cards (Mastercard, Wise debit) work on Alipay. However, payments above RMB 200 may incur a 3% transaction fee. For large bills, consider splitting payments or using cash.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="tip-card">
        <div class="tip-icon">🏨</div>
        <div class="tip-title">Stay Near Train Stations</div>
        <p class="tip-text">I prioritised hotels near railway stations. With a large suitcase, this saved enormous energy between cities. Comfort over hostel savings was absolutely the right call.</p>
