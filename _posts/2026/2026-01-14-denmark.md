  <h3>Royal Traditions</h3>
  <p>
    <strong>Amalienborg Palace</strong> is the official residence of the Danish royal family. It’s an impressive octagonal courtyard that feels grand yet accessible.
  </p>

  <div class="info-box tip">
    <strong>💂 Travel Tip: Changing of the Guard</strong><br>
    The Royal Life Guards arrive at Amalienborg at <strong>12:00 PM</strong>. For the full experience, be there around <strong>11:30 AM</strong> to watch the parade leaving from Rosenborg Castle.
  </div>

  <div class="spot-highlight">
    <img src="/assets/img/2026/denmark/apotek57.jpeg" alt="Apotek 57 Pancakes" class="spot-img">
    <div class="spot-text">
      <span class="spot-meta">Lunch • Frederiksstaden</span>
      <h3>Apotek 57</h3>
      <p>I ordered the seasonal pancakes. Let's talk about the price: <strong>DKK 150.72</strong>.</p>
      <p>This is officially the most expensive pancake I have ever eaten in my life (LOL). It was good, but perhaps a bit "too classic" to justify the price.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="spot-highlight reverse">
    <img src="/assets/img/2026/denmark/IMG_6485.JPG" alt="The Black Diamond" class="spot-img">
    <div class="spot-text">
      <span class="spot-meta">The Royal Library</span>
      <h3>The Black Diamond</h3>
      <p>More than just a library, this is a modern architectural masterpiece leaning over the water. It features free Wi-Fi, open reading rooms, and a cafe.</p>
      <p><em>It was the perfect modern contrast to the historic morning.</em></p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="info-box tip">
    <strong style="text-transform: uppercase; font-size: 0.85rem; display: block; margin-bottom: 10px;">💡 Luggage Hack</strong>
    <p style="margin:0; font-size: 0.95rem;">
      Need to store bags? Use the <strong>Black Diamond lockers</strong>. Totally free!
    </p>
    <br>
    <p><strong>Small Lockers:</strong> Level 0, left of entrance.</p>
    <p><strong>Large Lockers:</strong> Level 1, under the stairs. Fits carry-on suitcases.</p>
