    <div class="category-tag amber-tag">💎 Sightseeing</div>
    <span class="place-title">Mariacka Street (Amber Street)</span>
    <p>I wandered down this beautiful historic street to hunt for amber. It is filled with jewelry shops and historic charm. While the big amber pieces are pricey, the smaller souvenirs are reasonable.</p>
    <p><strong>Note:</strong> It never felt overwhelming. There was plenty of space to browse and take urban photos.</p>

    <p>Right in the heart of the city. It depicts the Roman god of the sea. There were a few street performers around — definitely worth a short stop if you are nearby.</p>

    <span class="place-title">Mr. & Mrs. Coffee</span>

    <p><em>The Highlight:</em> The owner was incredibly warm. We chatted for a long time (classic me!), and she even recommended where to buy affordable amber locally.</p>
