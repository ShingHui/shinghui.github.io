<div style="background: #fffafa; border: 1px solid #e2e8f0; border-radius: 12px; padding: 20px; margin: 30px 0;">
  <h3 id="bus-tip" style="margin-top: 0; color: #4a5568; font-size: 1.2rem;">🚌 Which Stop Should You Choose in CK?</h3>
  <p>FlixBus has two stops in Český Krumlov. Depending on your luggage and what you want to see first, here’s the breakdown:</p>
  
  <div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(250px, 1fr)); gap: 15px;">
    <div style="background: #f0fff4; padding: 15px; border-radius: 8px; border: 1px solid #c6f6d5;">
      <strong style="color: #2f855a;">📍 Option A: Špičák (The Shortcut)</strong>
      <p style="font-size: 0.85rem; margin-top: 5px;">Best if you have heavy luggage or are staying near the <strong>Castle District</strong>. It’s a flat, quick walk into the heart of the historic area.</p>
    </div>

    <div style="background: #ebf8ff; padding: 15px; border-radius: 8px; border: 1px solid #bee3f8;">
      <strong style="color: #2b6cb0;">📍 Option B: AN Terminal (The Scenic Route)</strong>
      <p style="font-size: 0.85rem; margin-top: 5px;">This is where I started! It’s closer to the <strong>Svornosti Square</strong>. While it involves some uphill/downhill walking with bags, you get to see more of the town's outskirts and river views right away.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <p style="font-size: 0.9rem; margin-top: 15px; font-style: italic; color: #718096;">
    <strong>My experience:</strong> I walked from AN Terminal and found it a great way to soak in the town.
  </p>
  <p style="font-size: 0.9rem; background: #fff; padding: 10px; border-radius: 6px; margin-top: 15px;">
    <strong>💡 Pro Tip:</strong> You board the bus for your return to Prague at the <em>exact same spot</em> where you got off at Špičák. Don’t cross the street! 
  </p>
</div>

<h2 id="day-1">October 26, 2024: Old Town Exploration</h2>
